About Mission Vision Core Values

Why the Name "Error Tracker"?

The name Error Tracker represents our commitment to identifying, analyzing, and solving problems in the field of Information Systems. Just like a system that detects and tracks errors to improve performance, we believe that mistakes are opportunities for growth and innovation. We do not avoid errors — we track them, understand them, and turn them into meaningful solutions.

Mission

To develop innovative and reliable information system solutions that address real-world challenges while continuously improving our technical skills, teamwork, and ethical responsibility as future IT professionals.

Vision

To become competent and globally competitive Information Systems professionals who create efficient, secure, and impactful technology solutions for businesses and communities.

Core Values

  • Integrity – Upholding honesty and ethical standards.
  • Service – Using knowledge and skills to serve the community.
  • Unity – Promoting teamwork and collaboration.
  • Excellence – Striving for continuous improvement.
  • Responsibility – Being accountable in every project.
